Image to PDF FAQ

How does image reordering work?
After uploading images, thumbnail previews will appear in a grid. You can click the arrow buttons (◀ and ▶) on each thumbnail to shift pages left or right, organizing the exact sequence of your PDF pages before clicking compile.
Is there a page limit?
There is no hard limit on the number of pages. However, compiling dozens of high-resolution images can consume significant memory. For best performance, we recommend converting up to 50 photos at a time.
Can I mix JPG and PNG files?
Yes! Our PDF compiler reads each file format independently, compresses the raw pixels, and writes them cleanly into separate pages of a single PDF file.
