HEIC Converter FAQ

What is a HEIC file?
HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's standard photo compression format for iOS. It saves high-quality images at half the file size of JPG, but it is not natively supported by most Windows platforms and web utilities.
How does client-side HEIC conversion work?
We load a lightweight decoding library (`heic2any`) via CDN directly in your browser. When you select HEIC files, this library decodes the Apple-encoded picture buffer and translates it to standard PNG/JPEG output. This does not use any internet bandwidth since no files are uploaded to servers.
Is it safe to convert private photos here?
Yes. Because all photo processing occurs locally on your own computer, your personal photos are never shared, uploaded, or exposed to any website servers or database networks.
