PDF Compressor FAQ

How does local PDF compression work?
If a PDF contains high-resolution scanned pages or graphics, it can be extremely bulky. Our tool uses PDF.js to render each page onto a canvas, rescales it based on your chosen level (Low/Medium/High compression), adjusts JPEG compression ratios, and packages it into a brand new optimized PDF using jsPDF.
Will text-based PDFs compress?
This local canvas-based compressor is highly optimized for **scanned PDFs, invoices, receipts, and image-heavy documents**. Text-only PDFs (like pure ebooks or word exports) are already very small and might not see significant compression.
Is the quality of my PDF compromised?
Medium compression yields a perfect balance where text remains clear and readable while cutting the size by 40-70%. High compression is best when you need to meet strict upload limits (like 2MB portals) and can tolerate slightly softer images.
