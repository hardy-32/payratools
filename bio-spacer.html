Bio Spacer FAQ

Why does Instagram collapse my bio line breaks?
Instagram's styling parser automatically trims trailing white spaces and collapses consecutive empty lines to save vertical screen space. This ruins formatted bullet points or spacing in captions and bios.
How does the invisible space character work?
We inject a special unicode character called the Braille Pattern Blank (`⠀` - character code U+2800). Because it is not considered a standard white space character by Instagram, it is not stripped, but it renders completely invisibly in browsers and applications, preserving your line break structure.
What is the character limit for Instagram bios?
Instagram bios are strictly limited to **150 characters**. Our character counter will turn red if your bio exceeds this limit, helping you format it perfectly before copying.
